Our focus

Some students in New Zealand can’t access out-of-school learning support. We’re here to help reduce that gap — one session at a time.

About us

We’re a student-led organisation based in Auckland. Our real-world issue is simple: not every student can access extra learning outside school. We provide tutoring across multiple subjects to support confidence, skill-building, and curiosity.

Our mission

Make out-of-school support easier to access for primary to intermediate learners through a friendly, consistent tutoring experience.

Our values

  • Kindness + patience
  • Clear explanations
  • Confidence-building
  • Learning that feels possible

Where we work

Auckland-based. Sessions can be arranged in agreed safe public spaces (e.g., libraries) or online — depending on what families prefer.
